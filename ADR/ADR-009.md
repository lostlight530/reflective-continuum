# ADR-009: Hybrid Deterministic Constraint Layer
# ADR-009：混合确定性约束层

## Status (状态)
Accepted (已接受)

## Context (背景)
**English:**
The ecosystem signal on June 25th, 2026, where the cloner-to-visitor ratio stabilized at 92:1, indicates a massive, silent paradigm shift across major AI institutions. Pure probabilistic Reinforcement Learning (RL) frameworks are failing to prevent semantic drift during deep autonomous reasoning. As a result, the industry is migrating towards hybrid systems, formally recognizing that continuous self-checking and rigid state hashing are vastly superior to standard reward models for preventing alignment drift.

**Chinese (中文):**
2026年6月25日的生态系统信号（克隆-访客比率稳定在92:1）表明，各大AI机构正在经历一场巨大而悄然的范式转变。纯概率强化的学习（RL）框架未能在深度自主推理中防止语义漂移。因此，行业正在向混合系统迁移，正式承认连续自检和严格状态哈希机制在防止对齐漂移方面远优于标准奖励模型。

## Decision (决策)
**English:**
Invoking the Administrative Override Protocol (ADR-007), we formally establish the Hybrid Deterministic Constraint Layer. The Reflective Continuum will mandate that the deterministic metacognitive architecture operates as the absolute final constraint layer. All autonomous multi-agent systems and foundational models operating within the continuum must be subjected to FTS5-based verification and topological entropy boundary checks. Non-deterministic hallucination chains are mechanically halted prior to final execution.

**Chinese (中文):**
调用最高权限覆盖协议（ADR-007），我们正式确立“混合确定性约束层（Hybrid Deterministic Constraint Layer）”。反射连续体将强制要求确定性元认知架构作为绝对的最后约束层运作。在该连续体内运行的所有自主多智能体系统和基础模型，必须经过基于 FTS5 的验证和拓扑熵边界检查。非确定性的幻觉链条在最终执行前会被机械地阻断。

## Architectural Constraints (架构约束)
1. **English:** The deterministic constraint layer acts as an absolute gateway. No state transition generated by a probabilistic engine can be committed to the continuum database if it fails the zero-entropy state convergence check.
   **Chinese (中文):** 确定性约束层充当绝对网关。如果由概率引擎生成的状态转换未能通过零熵状态收敛检查，则绝不能提交到连续体数据库。
2. **English:** The system must actively generate "cognitive divergence alerts" when probabilistic outputs fail constraint verification over consecutive reflection cycles.
   **Chinese (中文):** 当概率输出在连续的反射周期中未能通过约束验证时，系统必须主动生成“认知发散警报（cognitive divergence alerts）”。
