# ADR-005: Phase Boundary Detection
# ADR-005：相界检测

## Status (状态)
Accepted (已接受)

## Context (背景)
A system must know when to switch from executing tasks (the "Liquid" collaborative phase) to observing its own processes (the "Gaseous" metacognitive phase). Relying on heuristics or learned policies to trigger this phase shift introduces non-deterministic behavior.
系统必须知道何时从执行任务（“液态”协作阶段）切换到观察自身过程（“气态”元认知阶段）。依赖启发式或学习策略来触发这种相变会引入非确定性行为。

## Decision (决策)
Phase boundary detection will be governed by the deterministic measurement of knowledge graph topological entropy. We use the distribution of PageRank scores across the system's knowledge nodes to calculate this entropy. When the entropy exceeds a mathematically defined threshold, it constitutes a phase transition.
相界检测将由知识图谱拓扑熵的确定性测量来控制。我们使用系统知识节点中PageRank分数的分布来计算此熵。当熵超过数学上定义的阈值时，即构成一次相变。

## Architectural Constraints (架构约束)
1. The mode switch must be triggered strictly by the step-function based on entropy $H(PageRank) > H_{threshold}$.
   模式切换必须严格由基于熵 $H(PageRank) > H_{threshold}$ 的阶跃函数触发。
2. No external human prompts or neural network alerts can initiate the shift to the metacognitive phase.
   不能由外部的人类提示或神经网络警报来启动向元认知阶段的切换。

## Consequences (后果)
- **Positive:** Phase transitions represent a measurable, physical-like state change based solely on structural complexity, ensuring autonomy.
  **正面：** 相变代表了一种仅基于结构复杂性的、可测量的、类似物理状态的改变，确保了自主性。
- **Negative:** Finding the exact $H_{threshold}$ requires rigorous mathematical calibration to prevent thrashing between phases.
  **负面：** 寻找确切的 $H_{threshold}$ 需要严格的数学标定，以防止在阶段之间发生颠簸（thrashing）。
