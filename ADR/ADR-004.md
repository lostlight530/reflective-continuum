# ADR-004: Self-Consistency Verification
# ADR-004：自洽性验证

## Status (状态)
Accepted (已接受)

## Context (背景)
When a system reflects and attempts to correct itself, the correction mechanism itself may introduce new errors (probabilistic self-correction). For deterministic metacognition, proposed state changes must not contradict the fundamental rules defining the system's architecture.
当一个系统进行反思并试图纠正自己时，纠正机制本身可能会引入新的错误（概率性自我纠正）。对于确定性元认知，提出的状态变更绝不能与定义系统架构的基本规则相矛盾。

## Decision (决策)
Every proposed output or state mutation must be deterministically validated against the system's own corpus of formal constraints (the Architectural Decision Records). If any contradiction occurs, the system must perform a hard rollback. It enforces constraints rather than "rethinking" them.
每一个拟议的输出或状态突变，都必须针对系统自身的形式化约束语料库（架构决策记录）进行确定性验证。如果发生任何矛盾，系统必须执行硬回滚。它只执行约束，而不是去“重新思考”它们。

## Architectural Constraints (架构约束)
1. The constraint corpus (ADRs) must be readable and parsable by the system itself.
   约束语料库（ADR）必须能被系统自身读取和解析。
2. Verification relies purely on logical constraint satisfaction (boolean outcome).
   验证纯粹依赖于逻辑约束满足（布尔结果）。

## Consequences (后果)
- **Positive:** Ensures that the system cannot drift from its foundational architectural principles over time.
  **正面：** 确保系统随着时间推移不会偏离其基础架构原则。
- **Negative:** Strict enforcement may reject novel but technically constraint-violating solutions that a human engineer might temporarily tolerate.
  **负面：** 严格的执行可能会拒绝那些新颖但在技术上违反约束的解决方案，而这些方案人类工程师可能会暂时容忍。
