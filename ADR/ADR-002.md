# ADR-002: Reflection Depth Constraint

## Status (状态)
Accepted (已接受)

## Context (背景)
Self-reflective systems carry the inherent risk of infinite recursion—evaluating their evaluation of an evaluation indefinitely. Current trends in Test-Time Compute encourage unbounded scaling of inference, leading to computational inflation without guaranteed convergence.
自我反思的系统带有固有的无限递归风险——即无止境地评估其自身的评估。当前测试时计算（Test-Time Compute）的趋势鼓励无界限的推理扩展，这导致了计算资源的通胀且无法保证收敛。

## Decision (决策)
We constrain the metacognitive recursive depth strictly by a deterministic constant $N$. If the system does not converge on a stable state within $N$ layers of reflection, it must immediately halt reflection, register a "Cognitive Rejection" event, and roll back to the last known stable state.
我们严格使用确定性常数 $N$ 来约束元认知的递归深度。如果在 $N$ 层反思内系统未能收敛到一个稳定状态，它必须立即停止反思，记录一个“认知拒绝（Cognitive Rejection）”事件，并回滚到上一个已知的稳定状态。

## Architectural Constraints (架构约束)
1. A hard limit counter must be implemented for all introspective loops.
   所有内省循环必须实现一个硬性限制计数器。
2. The system must not attempt to dynamically expand compute budgets when a constraint violation is detected during reflection.
   在反思过程中检测到约束违规时，系统不得尝试动态扩展计算预算。

## Consequences (后果)
- **Positive:** Systematically rejects unbounded computational inflation. Guarantees termination of all cognitive loops.
  **正面：** 系统性地拒绝无底洞的算力通胀。保证所有认知循环必定终止。
- **Negative:** Extremely complex edge cases might trigger Cognitive Rejection rather than being resolved given more "thinking" time.
  **负面：** 极其复杂的边缘情况可能会触发认知拒绝，而不是在获得更多“思考”时间后得到解决。
