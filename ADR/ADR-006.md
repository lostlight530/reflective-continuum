# ADR-006: Orthogonal Coexistence with Probabilistic Paradigms
# ADR-006：与概率范式的正交共存

## Status (状态)
Accepted (已接受)

## Context (背景)
In defining the deterministic architecture of the Reflective Continuum, we must clarify our relationship with dominant probabilistic paradigms—specifically Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Few-Shot Learning. Modern AI relies heavily on RL for policy evolution through trial-and-error gradients, and Few-Shot Learning for in-context pattern matching and generalization. A common misconception is that a deterministic metacognitive system seeks to replace or obsolete these probabilistic mechanisms.
在定义反射连续体的确定性架构时，我们必须澄清我们与占主导地位的概率范式（特别是强化学习 RL 和小样本学习）之间的关系。现代 AI 严重依赖强化学习通过试错梯度进行策略演进，并依赖小样本学习进行上下文模式匹配和泛化。一个常见的误解是：确定性元认知系统试图取代或淘汰这些概率机制。

## Decision (决策)
The Reflective Continuum explicitly does **not** replace Reinforcement Learning or Few-Shot Learning. Instead, it operates orthogonally to them, residing in a higher abstraction layer.
反射连续体明确表示**不**取代强化学习或小样本学习。相反，它与它们正交运作，驻留在更高的抽象层上。

We define a strict decoupling:
我们定义了严格的解耦：
1. **Execution Layer (The Liquid Phase - Probabilistic):** RL and Few-Shot algorithms operate here. They are free to explore, hallucinate, generalize, and converge probabilistically. They are the engine of adaptation.
   **执行层（液态 - 概率性）：** 强化学习和小样本算法在此运作。它们可以自由地探索、产生幻觉、泛化并进行概率性收敛。它们是适应的引擎。
2. **Observation Layer (The Gaseous Phase - Deterministic):** The Reflective Continuum operates here. It does not dictate *how* the execution layer explores; it merely measures the exact topological state changes resulting from that exploration and enforces absolute boundaries via formal constraints.
   **观察层（气态 - 确定性）：** 反射连续体在此运作。它不干涉执行层*如何*探索；它仅测量由这种探索导致的精确拓扑状态变化，并通过形式化约束强制执行绝对的边界。

## Architectural Constraints (架构约束)
1. The metacognitive protocols defined in this repository must not attempt to calculate gradients, define reward functions, or parse few-shot examples.
   本仓库定义的元认知协议不得试图计算梯度、定义奖励函数或解析小样本示例。
2. The deterministic protocols must treat the output of probabilistic systems purely as a state proposal (a knowledge graph snapshot). They evaluate the *result* structurally, completely ignoring the probabilistic *method* that generated it.
   确定性协议必须将概率系统的输出纯粹视为一种状态提案（知识图谱快照）。它们在结构上评估*结果*，完全忽略生成该结果的概率性*方法*。

## Consequences (后果)
- **Positive:** Enables perfect coexistence. The system can leverage the immense exploratory power of RL and the contextual adaptability of Few-Shot Learning, while guaranteeing mathematical safety and self-consistency from a level above.
  **正面：** 实现了完美的共存。系统可以利用强化学习巨大的探索能力和小样本学习的上下文适应性，同时从更高的层级保证数学上的安全性和自洽性。
- **Negative:** The deterministic layer remains blind to the "intent" or "confidence scores" of the underlying neural models, treating all outputs simply as unverified state data until mathematically proven.
  **负面：** 确定性层对底层神经模型的“意图”或“置信度得分”保持盲目，仅将所有输出视为未经验证的状态数据，直到被数学证明为止。

## 4. Constraint Layer Mechanism (约束层机制)
**English:**
Current industry analysis reveals a critical migration: leading AI players are quietly moving away from pure probabilistic Reinforcement Learning (RL) towards hybrid systems. In our continuum, deterministic metacognition acts as the absolute final constraint layer (Hard Rollback), entirely discarding standard reward models which are susceptible to catastrophic hallucination chains.

**Chinese:**
当前的行业分析揭示了一个关键迁移：领先的AI玩家正悄然从纯粹的概率性强化学习（RL）转向混合系统。在我们的连续体中，确定性元认知作为绝对的最终约束层（硬回滚），彻底摒弃了容易导致灾难性幻觉链的标准奖励模型。
